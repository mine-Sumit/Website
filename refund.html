Refund Policy

At Horizon, we prioritize transparency, integrity, and client satisfaction in all our dealings. We understand that circumstances may arise where a client seeks a refund, and we are committed to addressing such situations promptly and fairly. Our refund policy is designed to ensure that clients are treated equitably while also safeguarding the financial stability of our operations.

 

In the event of a refund request, the core capital invested by the client will be accorded the highest priority. This means that any refund will first aim to return the initial capital without considering the profits generated over it. If the client has outstanding charges or debts to our company, these amounts will be deducted from the refund before it is initiated.

 

Clients may be eligible for a refund under various circumstances, including but not limited to -

 

  • Dissatisfaction with the service provided by Horizon.
  • Technical issues that hinder the client's ability to access or utilize our services effectively.
  • Failure of our investment strategies to meet agreed-upon performance benchmarks.

 

 

At Horizon, we believe in aligning our interests with those of our clients. To this end, we do not charge any fees unless we have generated a minimum return of 5% - 10% over the client's capital. This ensures that our clients only pay for our services when they see tangible gains in their investments.

 

Clients seeking a refund are required to submit a formal request detailing the reasons for the refund and any supporting documentation. We will review the request promptly and communicate the decision to the client in a timely manner. If the refund is approved, the amount will be processed and disbursed according to the prioritization outlined above.

At Horizon, we value the trust and confidence our clients place in us. Our refund policy is designed to uphold our commitment to fairness, accountability, and client satisfaction. We strive to resolve any refund requests efficiently and amicably, ensuring that our clients' interests are always paramount.
